About Us

Our Story

XPBooster was founded in 2020 out of a passion for Old School RuneScape and a desire to help players achieve their in-game goals. What started as a small team of expert players has grown into a trusted service for thousands of players worldwide.

Our founder, a RuneScape veteran with over 15 years of experience, identified the need for a professional, secure, and reliable service that would allow players to enjoy the game more while reaching their goals.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide the highest quality services while maintaining account security as our top priority. We believe everyone should be able to enjoy Old School RuneScape, regardless of their available time or skill level.

We strive to make the OSRS experience more enjoyable and accessible by offering professional services that save players time and help them achieve their in-game ambitions.
